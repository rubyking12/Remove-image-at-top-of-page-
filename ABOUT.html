About

Coding for Girls is a platform designed to inspire and encourage girls to pursue coding at school, university or as a hobby.
This website serves as a data base to learn the history of coding and how to code, play games, enter into competitions and be a part of an exclusive community, specifically, for girls.
Coding for girls promotes creativity and problem solving through monthly competitions. Winners are selected by industry professionals and the winners each recieve an exclusive prize.
This website was formed on the basis of addressing the adamant need of more women in technology fields! This platform hopes to inspire young girls to challenge the expected norm and find their passion through coding.


Founder

Ruby King

Ruby King is 21 year's old and is currently undertaking a Bachelor in Communications, majoring in Digital & Social Media and Social & Political Science. She is an advocate for gender equality and sees the value of education in breaking free from society's expected stereotype of women. The aim of the website is to educate young girls on the endless possibilities of coding and equip them with a skill for life.
